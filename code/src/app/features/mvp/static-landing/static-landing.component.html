Eliminating factors that hinder individuals from living proactively

Our goal is to identify and reevaluate the factors that hinder individuals from living proactively. By doing so, we aim to redefine these factors, making it easier for individuals to live in this world.

The situation what I want to change...

I am currently in the job-hunting process. However, due to a lack of skills and experience, I am not accepted by companies no matter how many times I apply, and I cannot afford to go to university because of a lack of funds. Even when I plan to take other actions, my lack of capital limits my ability to act. Even if I try to consult with someone about this situation, I don't have reliable friends to rely on. Why can't I take action even though I want to do something?

I'm not happy with this situation. Even though I want to improve the current situation, I cannot. In other words, it is evidence that the system in this world has flaws.

The goal is to create an environment where individuals, when they wish to change the current situation, can implement improvements without being hindered by anything.

Target

We will provide an environment where individuals can acquire the necessary skills and experiences to live in society, regardless of interpersonal relationships or the presence of capital. We aim to create a setting where individuals can, based on their efforts, attain happiness independently.

No matter how much effort is exerted, without rewards, lacking capital to take action, and with no one to support, individuals may find themselves burdened with loneliness. For those people, we exist.

The ability to receive the materials and services necessary for taking action without any constraints.

The ability to easily build the necessary interpersonal relationships for taking action.

An environment where individuals can experiment and learn, regardless of the presence of capital, to acquire skills and experiences through trial and error.

Business Model

Setting people's concerns as quests and forming teams to solve problems

System

Based on the hypothesis that, with mutual consent, people can engage in economic activities without using currency for all goods and services

Afterward, compile the obtained results and share them with society, benefiting people facing similar problems. Costs only arise when the results are commercially used. Profits obtained are distributed to the team. The cost becomes the capital for individuals who want to gain experience and skills to take action.

Managers

Manage the progress of the team's project. Assist in compiling project results. The entire system is defined as one team.

Manage the project and sell results to the Company.

Improvers

Collaborate with strangers in teams to solve the problems and challenges participants bring. In the improvement process, they engage in trial and error, aiming to acquire skills and experiences.

Provide issues as quests to Participants and receiving a feedback as compensation.

Participants

Offer their problems and challenges to others. In exchange for free solutions, they provide thorough feedback to the improvers.

Acquire one's own competence and experience through the quests.

Company

Pays the profits to the team and the manager, then profits shares to the team.

Benefits

People who join the team can gain the following benefits

Improvers

  • Can use the materials and services needed for trial and error without any restrictions.
  • Can acquire the skills and experiences necessary for living in society, regardless of their current environment.
  • Can receive feedback from other companies.

Participants

Regardless of the presence of capital, they can have people improve issues for them.

Common benefits

Through collaborative work within the team, they can build relationships.

In conclusion

Our world's society is rapidly advancing, and things we cannot comprehend are increasing.

To survive in society, skills and experience are deemed essential, and those who cannot understand them are left behind. Let's reevaluate these notions and create an environment where everyone can obtain what is necessary to live in society.
